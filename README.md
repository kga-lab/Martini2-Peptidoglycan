This project repository contains gromacs itp/gro/forcefield files for model peptidoglycan in MARTINI-2 framework.
This peptidoglycan model has been developed by Dr. Rakesh K. Vaiwala and jointly published by 
Rakesh Vaiwala, Pradyumn Sharma, Mrinalini Puranik, and K. Ganapathy Ayappa in JCTC. 
https://pubs.acs.org/doi/pdf/10.1021/acs.jctc.0c00539

%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%
$singlechain_8mer$ = folder containing topology and force field files for a Peptidoglycan chain.
$System details:$
no. of glycan chains = 1 (8 disaccharide long)
no. of CG beads = 136
%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%
$chains7$ = folder containing topology and force field files for the Peptidoglycan network comprised of 7 glycan chains.
$System details:$ 
no. of glycan chains = 7 (each 13 disaccharides long)
no. of CG beads = 1517
no. of cross-links = 30
%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%
$chains21$ = folder containing topology and force field files for the Peptidoglycan network comprised of 21 glycan chains.
$System details:$
no. of glycan chains = 21 
no. of CG beads = 5140
no. of cross-links = 96
%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%
$chains21_pmf$ = folder containing topology and force field files for the Peptidoglycan (relatively smaller patch size).
$System details:$
no. of glycan chains = 21 
no. of CG beads = 2098
no. of cross-links = 44

For more details Please refer our JCTC article at:
https://pubs.acs.org/doi/pdf/10.1021/acs.jctc.0c00539
